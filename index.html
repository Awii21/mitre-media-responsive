Who We Are

Mitre Media is a web-native financial media company focused on connecting sophisticated users with actionable solutions to their financial questions and needs. The team is led by industry veterans who have been in the online media game for decades.

Mitre Media is focused on financial professionals and high net worth investors. By providing tools and resources on properties such as ETFdb.com, Dividend.com and MunicipalBonds.com, Mitre Media is serious about providing actionable solutions to our users.

What Makes Us Different

Mitre provides a unique atmosphere because the environment, by design, is a fusion of hard core finance geeks, cutting edge technologists and savvy web marketers.

Each team member brings a unique and innovative perspective and skill set to help solve the problem for which Mitre Media is built to solve – connect sophisticated investors to sophisticated answers.

Team Depth

Mitre is fortunate to have a solid roster of advisors and directors that aid in the strategic decision making process and facilitate high value connections.

Similar to the philosophy applied to the core team, Mitre has focused on bringing together complimentary skill sets and expertise when choosing advisors and directors in an effort to bring multiple, different and accretive perspectives to challenges and opportunities faced by the company.
